Burger Dish

Description:

a popular sandwich made from ground meats that are formed into a patty,

cooked, and placed between two halves of a bun. and this burger is made with cow meat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Large egg
  • ½ Teaspoon salt
  • ½ Teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 Pound ground beef
  • ½ Cup fine dry bread crumps

Steps:

  1. Preheat an grill for high heat and lighly oil grate.
  2. Whisk together egg, salt, pepper, bread crumbs, and ground beef and mix!
  3. Place patties on the preheated grill, Cook 6 to 8 minutes each side.
    4. Should be Ready to eat, Enjoy!
